## 📋 STRUCTURED BRIEF

### 1. The core reward: leveling literally hands you reel edits
💡🔬 **The single strongest research fit.** Progression should resolve into **reel edits**, not abstract power
— fusing FF5/FFT job-ability-learning + FFX Sphere-Grid node-activation + D&D milestone forks **onto the
reel itself.** On level/milestone, pick from a small menu that all resolve to reel changes:
> *{ new face · upgrade a face's tier/multiplier · unlock the next reel-slot (toward 2–5) · upgrade a payline
> profile · a flat stat lever · an Ultimate upgrade }*
The progression screen **previews the reel before/after** each pick (Pillar 3). This is the literal
expression of "builds edit the reels."

### 2. Progression currency
❓ *What's earned and from what:* XP from combat? Reel-shards from bosses? Reel Points from levels (see
[[23-talents-and-reel-points]])? 💡 *Recommend: combat grants **XP → levels**; each level grants **Reel
Points** (the budget) + occasional **milestone forks** (below).*

### 3. Curve & pacing
💡🔬 **Encounter-paced, level-capped — not a grind knob.** Over-leveling would let players brute-force past
the spin (undermining Pillar 1). A flat-ish, campaign-paced curve fits "campaign-first." 🟦 *Level cap?*
All curve numbers `[ASSUMPTION]`.

### 4. Milestone choice forks (the memorable spikes)
💡🔬 **Every N levels, "Temper your Tools": pick ONE** — a new reel-slot (math/power) **OR** a unique
signature face (cool thing) **OR** +1 stat. Mirrors D&D's beloved ASI-vs-feat fork. Strictly-dominant options
forbidden (Pillar 4).

### 5. Portability rule
❓🔬 *FF5/FFT make mastered abilities portable across jobs.* For us: **are earned faces shared party-wide or
PC-locked?** 💡 *Recommend **PC-locked growth** (each character builds their own reels) but **gear/bank
items transferable** ([[26-banking-cross-character]]) — so identity is personal, loot is shared.*

### 6. Companion parity
✅ Companions level via this exact system ([[12-companions-and-party]] §5). ❓ Bench-XP rule lives there.

### 7. Anti-homogenization (Pillar 4)
💡 *What stops every character converging on one optimal reel set?* The **Reel Points budget** +
the **Resonance cap** on reel-affix gear ([[24-equipment]]) + class/weapon baselines that differ. Spell out here.

### 8. Respec policy
❓ *Can the player refund Reel Points / re-pick faces?* 💡 *Recommend respec at the Rest workbench for a cost
(coins or essence) — supports experimentation without trivializing choices.*

### 9. Reel-forging / recombination (WATCH-ITEM)
⏳🔬 *Persona-fusion's "recombine owned faces into a stronger face" verb is tempting but risks becoming a
**second build engine** that rivals reel-editing.* **Only build if reel-editing alone proves shallow in
playtest.** If adopted, it lives in [[27-crafting]] as a subordinate verb, hard-capped to editing existing
reels. *Flagged as the one proposal that could collide with Pillar 2 — validate need first.*

### 10. Legibility spec
✅ Progression UI shows the reel **before/after** every choice.

### 11. Open questions
- ❓ XP vs. milestone vs. hybrid. ❓ Level cap & curve. ❓ Shared vs. PC-locked face growth. ❓ Respec cost.

### Scope / phase
