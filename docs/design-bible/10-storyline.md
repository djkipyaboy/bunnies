## 💬 BRAIN DUMP (yours)

*Dump anything — premise, scenes you can already picture, the villain, the vibe, a line of dialogue,
a Redwall book you want to echo. Fragments are fine. Optional starter prompts:*

- 🟦 What is the **core conflict**? (woodlanders vs. a specific vermin threat? a corruption? a lost relic?)
- 🟦 What is the **PC's personal stake** at the start? (orphaned? exiled? chosen? reluctant?)
- 🟦 What's the **tone target** on the all-ages-with-real-stakes spectrum? (Mossflower-grim? lighter/adventurous?)
- 🟦 Roughly how **long / how many chapters** do you imagine for the campaign?
- 🟦 Any **themes** you want it to be *about* (belonging, redemption of vermin, found family, duty vs. freedom)?
- 🟦 Is the story **linear** or do you want **branching/choices** (KOTOR-style alignment, multiple endings)?

## 📋 STRUCTURED BRIEF *(I fill this from your dump)*

### 1. Premise & logline
🟦 *One-paragraph pitch of the campaign — who, what threat, what's at stake.*

### 2. Themes & tone
🟦 *The 2–3 themes; the tone target. Redwall touchstones to echo / avoid.*

### 3. Story structure
🟦 *Act/chapter shape.* 🔬 *The world research recommends a **hub + linear chapters** spine (Paper Mario /
classic FF), each chapter culminating in a designed boss — see [[11-world-and-overworld]] and
[[28-encounter-design-framework]].* ❓ Linear, or branching with **alignment & multiple endings** (KOTOR)?

### 4. Player-character framing
🟦 *Is the PC a blank-slate avatar the player names/creates (KOTOR/D&D), or a defined protagonist?* This is
the hinge with [[20-character-creation]]. 💡 *Given the KOTOR companion model you chose, a **created,
named PC** (blank-ish vessel) fits best — companions carry much of the authored personality.*

### 5. Companions as story (the KOTOR spine)
🔬 *KOTOR 1/2: recruitable companions each carry a personal quest line, world ties, and react to the PC.*
This brief OWNS the *narrative* of each companion; [[12-companions-and-party]] owns the *system*; the
actual cast lives in [[42-companion-roster]]. 🟦 *Who are the first companions you can already picture?*

### 6. Factions & the world's powers
🟦 *The orders/clans/vermin hordes/abbeys at play, and how they relate.* (Folded here for now per your call;
splits into its own file if the cast grows.)

### 7. Player agency & branching
❓ *Do choices matter mechanically?* 🔬 *KOTOR influence/alignment ties story choices to companion loyalty
and powers — see the influence proposal in [[12-companions-and-party]].* ⏳ *Multiple endings can be scoped
after the spine locks.*

### 8. Hooks into systems
🟦 *Where the story should grant mechanical things:* a Background's signature reel face
([[20-character-creation]]), boss weaknesses telegraphed in lore ([[28-encounter-design-framework]]),
quest rewards as reel-faces/Reel-Points ([[23-talents-and-reel-points]]).

### 9. Open questions
- ❓ Linear vs. branching/alignment (drives a LOT of downstream scope).
- ❓ Named-created PC vs. defined protagonist.
- ❓ Campaign length target (sets chapter count and content budget).

### Scope / phase
✅ *Campaign-first.* ⏳ *Roguelite framing, New Game+, multiple-ending branches = post-1.0 unless you pull
them forward.*
