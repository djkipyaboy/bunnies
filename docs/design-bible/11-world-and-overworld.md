## 📋 STRUCTURED BRIEF *(I fill this from your dump)*

### 1. World shape
💡🔬 **Recommended: hub + linear chapters** (Paper Mario's Rogueport→chapters; classic FF town/overworld/
dungeon triad). A Redwall **Abbey/waystation hub** connects to chapter "journeys," each ending on a designed
boss. Reasons: best fit for *campaign-first*, lets us *introduce one new combat idea per chapter*, keeps the
fluctuating-enemy-count variety paced rather than dumped. 🟦 *Confirm or steer toward open-world/other.*

### 2. The out-of-combat loop
💡🔬 **Shop / Rest / Quest-board triad** (FF towns + Paper Mario TTYD "Trouble Center"). The loop's job:
make the *next fight a build decision*. 💡 **"Rest = the build workbench"** — at a campfire/Abbey-bell you
save, restore resources, and **re-edit your reel loadout / spend Reel Points / swap party** (ties to
[[23-talents-and-reel-points]]). This concentrates "builds edit the reels" into a clear repeated beat.

### 3. Chapter framework
💡🔬 **One new encounter archetype per chapter** (a curriculum, not a dump): e.g. ch.1 type-chart exploit →
ch.2 adds/summons → ch.3 multi-part boss → ch.4 telegraphed counter-state → ch.5 regenerating shield →
ch.6 multi-phase capstone. Each archetype is defined in [[28-encounter-design-framework]]. 🟦 *How many chapters?*

### 4. World behavior between combat
🟦 *What's interactive in the field?* 💡🔬 *Optional stretch — **one overworld "field verb" per class**
(Paper Mario partner abilities): Warden moves earth, Seer reveals hidden things, Skirmisher reaches far
ledges — so party composition matters outside combat too. Start with ~3 verbs to cap authoring cost.*

### 5. Collectibles & side content
💡🔬 **Make exploration FEED THE REELS** (Paper Mario Star Pieces → buy badges; Shine Sprites → party
upgrades):
- 💡 **Hidden "Acorns/Star-Shards"** spent at a hub vendor on **new reel faces / damage-type symbols** —
  exploration widens your build vocabulary.
- 💡 **Threshold token → party upgrade**: every N "Friendship Tokens" (from sidequests/exploration) levels
  a chosen party member. Legible set-collection motivation.
- 💡🔬 **Quest-board rewards** pay coins, **Reel Points**, or a new reel face — side content loops back to the build.
- ⏳ **Optional post-campaign gauntlet** ("Trials of the Old Ones," à la Pit of 100 Trials) re-using every
  boss archetype at max tuning — natural home for the deferred difficulty settings.
